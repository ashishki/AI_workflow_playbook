# Procurement Terms Comparison

## Problem

Buyers compare supplier offers manually across price, delivery, payment terms,
and risk clauses.

## AI Opportunity

Upload 3-10 offers -> comparison table with citations -> buyer approval. AI
does not choose the supplier autonomously.

## Data Required

Supplier offers, contract templates, vendor master data, historical delivery
performance, currency/tax rules.

## Risk and HITL

Risk: wrong price, missed condition, hallucinated clause, vendor bias. Buyer
approves all comparisons and final decisions.

## Evaluation Plan

Correct comparison fields, hallucination rate, source citation precision,
missing-clause recall, p95 latency, and cost per comparison.

## MVP Scope

One document set, one category, structured comparison table, citations.

## Production Hardening

Vendor master integration, contract clause library, approval workflow, and
currency/tax validation.
